#1 ANTIOXIDANT

Idebenone is the analogue of CoQ10, it has powerful antioxidant properties resulting in less cell and tissue damages. Unlike vitamin and botanical antioxidants, Idebenone is a respiratory chain antioxidant, which can correct the signs of aging while offering the highest protection against harmful environmental free radical stress with an Environmental Protection Factor of EPF 95, the highest rating of all antioxidants tested.

About Antioxidants and Environmental Protection Factor (EPF)

Only antioxidants can scavenge free radicals to help prevent damage and premature aging of skin. EPF (Environmental Protection Factor) is the certification standard to assess the topical protection capacity of antioxidants to protect the skin from free radical mediated environmental oxidative stress.

How is Ultra V patented Idebenone unique?
